### Total Improvements:

#### Bundle Size:
- **Total Size**: 421.22 KB → 404.02 KB (-17.2 KB, -4.1%)

#### Visual Size:
- **Section Padding**: Reduced by 30-50% across all sections
- **Text Sizes**: Reduced by 10-20% throughout
- **Spacing**: Reduced by 25-40% (margins, gaps, padding)
- **Component Sizes**: Icons, badges, cards all 15-30% smaller
- **Overall Page Height**: Approximately 35-40% shorter

### Key Changes:

1. **Removed Stats Section** from hero and page flow

3. **Reduced all header margins** from `mb-16/20` to `mb-10/12`
4. **Reduced all grid gaps** by 25-40%
5. **Reduced all text sizes** by 1-2 scale steps
6. **Reduced all card padding** by 25-40%
