## Design System Applied

### Colors
- **White Background**: #ffffff
- **Light Gray Background**: #f5f5f7
- **Dark Background**: #1d1d1f (dark mode)
- **Apple Blue**: #0071e3 (primary actions)
- **Text Dark**: #1d1d1f
- **Text Gray**: #86868b
- **Border Light**: #d2d2d7
- **Border Dark**: #424245

### Typography
- **Font Family**: SF Pro Display (headings), SF Pro Text (body)
- **H1**: 48px, -0.009em, line-height 1.05, weight 600
- **H2**: 40px, -0.007em, line-height 1.1, weight 600
- **Body**: 17px, -0.003em, line-height 1.47059, weight 400
- **Small**: 14px, -0.003em, line-height 1.47059, weight 400

### Spacing
- **Container**: max-w-[980px] (Apple Store standard)
- **Section Padding**: py-20 (80px vertical)
- **Grid Gap**: 12px (tight Apple grid)
- **Card Padding**: p-8 to p-10

### Components
- **Border Radius**: 18px (Apple standard for cards)
- **Button Radius**: rounded-full (Apple style)
- **Input Radius**: rounded-xl (12px)
- **Hover Scale**: 1.02 (subtle)
- **Animation Easing**: cubic-bezier(0.28, 0, 0.63, 1)
